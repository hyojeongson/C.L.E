In today’s fast-paced society, listening to our bodies is something we neglect more and more. If we become sick, we let experts “fix” us, rarely taking into consideration how we could have prevented illness in the first place. We at Space10 think it’s about time we started thinking about and designing for how we can make body awareness an integrated part of our lives, in order to prevent diseases that affect both individuals and whole societies. We need to reconnect with the most natural and powerful part of being human - our bodies - and also learn to take responsibility for our own wellbeing.
